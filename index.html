How to use this

1. Find the sign you need (search by name or filter by category).

2. Click the sign to open it in Canva. The editor will jump straight to that page of the master template.

3. In Canva: File → Make a copy so you don't edit the master, then change text, save, and download as PNG/PDF for printing.

If you don't have access to the master template, ask Sam to share it with your Canva account.
